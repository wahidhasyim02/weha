About Us

Bringing Back the Charm of Vintage Coffeehouses

SeduhRasa was founded with a mission to serve Indonesia’s finest coffee while recreating the cozy, welcoming ambiance of vintage coffeehouses. We believe coffee is more than just a drink—it’s a way to strengthen relationships, share stories, and enjoy life’s meaningful little moments.

Beyond coffee, we also offer refreshing cold beverages and dishes made from high-quality local ingredients, bringing you authentic flavors that satisfy. Our café is also a cultural hub, regularly hosting activities such as book reviews, reading sessions, history seminars, poetry readings, and live music. SeduhRasa is not just a place to hang out; it’s a community space where knowledge and culture thrive.
